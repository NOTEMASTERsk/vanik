# Vision

## Objective

Define the long-term future of Vanik.

## Vision Statement

To become India's most trusted digital platform for neighborhood retail, enabling every kirana store to compete and grow in the digital economy.

## 1-Year Vision

- Launch Vanik in one neighborhood.
- Onboard 10 kirana stores.
- Reach 200 registered customers.
- Complete 500 successful orders.

## 3-Year Vision

- Expand to multiple cities.
- Build a trusted brand for local commerce.
- Serve thousands of kirana stores.

## 10-Year Vision

Become the operating system for India's neighborhood retail ecosystem.

## Success Looks Like

- Customers prefer Vanik for local shopping.
- Kirana stores increase their sales.
- Vanik becomes profitable and sustainable.
